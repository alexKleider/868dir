
                    868 DIRECTORY
                    =============

    Efforts are underway to resurrect the 868 Directory.
    
    If you'd like to be included please send an email to
                868directory@gmail.com
    and specify exactly what you want included. Be sure 
    to mention if you do not wish your email address to
    be published. 
    Available fields are first name, middle name, last name,
    suffix, phone, address, town, state, postal_code, country
    and email. At least one name is mandatory. All other fields
    are optional. Provide as many phone numbers as you wish.
    Four digit numbers will be assumed to be "415/868" numbers.
    Your email address will be included unless you specify
    otherwise.
    Bolinas, CA 94924 USA will be assumed for empty town, state,
    postal_code and country fields.

    We'll keep you posted as things progress.
